Donate & Upgrade

  • You must complete the steps below:

    • Donate the correct amount to the assigned account.

    • Submit the donation form for approval.

    • Wait for approval. Assigned upline will review your donation.

    • Please provide full details (including screenshot) of the transaction.

    • Upline will validate your donation form submission within 24 hours. You can contact your upline directly after that.

    • Upline has the right to Reject form submission if unable to verify transaction details. You can resubmit if got rejected.

    • Communication about the upgrade process is between you and the upline.

    • If your account reach expiry, but your donation is still pending for approval, your account won't be removed by the system.

    • You have to read and agreed the iCharity Club terms here.

    • All donations are voluntarily and final. Refund not available.
